Common questions

What people ask before starting.

What is psychosomatic coaching?

Psychosomatic coaching addresses the connection between psychological stress and physical symptoms. When pressure goes unresolved, it manifests in the body as tension, disrupted sleep, and exhaustion. The work addresses root patterns in the nervous system and subconscious — not just the surface symptoms.

Who is this for?

Founders, CEOs, entrepreneurs, and high-achieving professionals — typically in their 30s, 40s, or 50s — who are primary financial providers experiencing chronic pressure, burnout symptoms, or physical stress responses that productivity and mindset work haven't resolved.

How is this different from therapy or regular coaching?

Coaching is forward-focused and performance-oriented. This work specifically targets how pressure lives in the body and shapes behaviour — using somatic techniques, nervous system regulation, and subconscious pattern work. It is not a substitute for therapy, and is designed for high-functioning individuals eroding under sustained pressure.

Can this help with burnout recovery?

Yes. Burnout requires nervous system regulation and addressing the underlying patterns driving over-extension — not just rest. The work here helps high achievers recover physiologically, build resilience, and prevent recurrence without sacrificing performance.

What is nervous system regulation?

The ability to return to a calm, clear baseline after stress — and to hold that baseline under sustained pressure. Dysregulated nervous systems drive poor decisions, physical symptoms, and relationship strain. Regulation work uses somatic practices and pattern interruption to build long-term physiological resilience.
