What this is

A diagnostic, not a quiz.

It identifies your dominant pressure pattern.

Pressure doesn't look the same in everyone. For some it shows up as over-responsibility. For others, control. Fear of collapse. Perfectionism. Emotional suppression. The audit names which pattern is most active for you right now.

It shows you where it lives in the body.

Psychosomatic work starts with location. Chest tightness, jaw clenching, shallow breathing, gut tension - these are data points, not side effects. The audit helps you map yours.

It gives you a starting point.

Not a general wellness score. A specific pattern you can work with - whether that's on your own, through the free challenge, or inside a private coaching session.
