Why a daily email?

Because change happens in the small moments - not the big plans.

Most "stress relief" advice asks you to overhaul your life. This challenge does the opposite. Every day you'll get one tiny shift: small enough that you'll actually do it, real enough that by Day 3 something will have moved.

The 3 Days

What you'll get in your inbox

Each email is a 2-minute read with one simple practice for that day.

  • Day 1 - Naming the pressure. A short audit of where the pressure lives in your week.
  • Day 2 - A nervous system reset. A 5-minute regulation tool you can do at your desk.
  • Day 3 - Your stability rhythm. A simple template to make this last beyond the three days.
What to expect

Three things this challenge is - and isn't.

Short.

Each email is a 2-minute read with one practice. No long PDFs, no homework you'll feel guilty about.

Doable.

Designed for real, busy lives. If you have 5 minutes, you have time for the day's practice.

Honest.

No miracle promises. Just real, evidence-informed shifts that compound when you stay with them.
